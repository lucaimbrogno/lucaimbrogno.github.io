What We Do.

Let us know what you're looking for! We have experience with everything from walls and ceilings to siding and fencing.

Interior.

Our bread and butter! We've completed a countless number of interior jobs to perfection. We spice up the look and feel of your home with the colours of your choice.

Exterior.

We have an excellent exterior team who's number one goal is to make your home look more alive from the outside in.

Commercial.

If you've got an office or store that needs a new look, we've got you covered! We have experience with turning dull, uninviting stores into bright and appealing establishments.

Who Are We.

We are a group of energetic friends who have been painting for years. After seeing the ineffective and unprofessional way many contractors interact with clients, we decided to start our own service. We strive to provide exceptional customer experience through responsive communication, on-time jobs and of course, a well-done job that lasts.

Our Process.

We always start with site preparation before we bring out the brushes. It's important to us that we respect your home by taking precautions with drop clothes and and plastic covers. Additionally, we believe great wall prep is the key to a successful job!

Our Approach.

We like to have a clear vision before we start painting. It's not about putting a bunch of new colours on a wall. It's about building the vision that you have in mind. Creating something new and exciting.

Meet The Team.

Tomas Tabak

Tomas has run a successful painting business for the last two years through the Student Works Painting business structure. After executing over 100 successful jobs, Tomas decided it was time to pursue his own independent painting service.

Luca Imbrogno

Shady Sedrak
